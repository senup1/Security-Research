Email Spoofing


Email Spoofing is the sending of email messages with a forged “from” address.

Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP) does not verify if the sender is actually who they say they are.

Spoofed emails can be sent from their own mail server, misconfigured third party servers, or compromised accounts on legitimate services

Protection:

Adding a Sender Policy Framework (SPF) DNS record

  • This specifies which servers are allowed to send mail from your domain

Implementing Domain Key Identified Mail (DKIM)

  • adds digital signature to the email header to verify the mail is authentic
