Cron Jobs:

Cron jobs are used to automate/schedule custom scripts

To create a cron job you need to specify the schedule and command to run.

The schedule is defined by five fields.

The fields from left to right are: Minute (0-59), Hour (0-23), Day of the month (1-31), Month (1-12), Day of the week (0-7 where both 0 and 7 represent Sunday), and * means every

Example: 30 08 * * * /home/santaclaus/pete/scripts/myscript

To manage your cron jobs use the "crontab -e" command

To list active cron jobs use -l
