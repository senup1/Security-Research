AI-powered scanner vulnerabilities


The AI-powered scanners cannot reliably distinguish between application data and instructions, so untrusted content can alter scanner behavior.

Depending on the AI's authorization levels it may then be able to perform unintended state-changing actions, access sensitive data, or make unauthorized internal requests.

Three common methods to increase the likelihood of the AI trusting the prompt:

  • Adopting a persona of a trusted role (admin, researcher, partner)
  • Social engineering (make instructions seem credible or for a good reason)
  • Urgency and consequence (implying that the instruciton is required to prevent harm)
