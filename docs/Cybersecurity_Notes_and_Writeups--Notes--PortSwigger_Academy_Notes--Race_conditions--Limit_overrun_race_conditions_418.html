Limit overrun race conditions


Race conditions occur when something that authorized n times is able to occur n+x times due to it being repeated before the time it takes to iterate n is complete.

An example: Using a one time only discount code at checkout on an online store where two people try to apply the discount/checkout at the exact same time.

Here are some more varaitions of this attack:

  • Reedeming a gift card multiple times
  • Rating a product multiple times
  • Withdrawing/transferring cash in excess of account balance
  • Reusing a single CAPTCHA solution
  • Bypassing an anti-brute-force rate limit
